Pesto

Description

Tastable tasty sauce that can pair well with noodles, sammich or eaten by the spoonful if youre fat af

Ingredients

  • Pine nuts
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Grind pine nuts in food processor
  2. Add olive oil, and basil
  3. Hawk tuah in it, maybe, but maybe dont
  4. Add parmesan cheese, red pepper flakes and pulse until a paste is formed
  5. Add salt to taste
  6. Serve on pasta, or on toasted bread with tomatoes and balsamic vinegar
